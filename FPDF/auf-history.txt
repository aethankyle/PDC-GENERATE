Angeles University Foundation admissions offer a world-class educational institute and is committed to excellence in research, instruction and community service. Angeles University Foundation ranking is at 35th best university in the Philippines in 2021.

AUF was incorporated under Republic Act No. 6055, otherwise known as the Foundation Law, and became a tax-exempt institution approved by the Philippine government. All donations and bequests given to the AUF are tax deductible. On February 14, 1978, AUF was converted to a Catholic University.
